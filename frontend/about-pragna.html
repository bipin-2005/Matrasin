Know Pragna's Story

The Girl Who Learned To Fight

A story not of weight loss, but of strength gained, purpose discovered, and resilience built through life's greatest challenges.

Learning To Fight

As a teenager, Pragna was diagnosed with Peripheral Neuropathy. While others planned their futures, she struggled to stand on her own feet and endured months of therapy.

Discovering Nutrition

Determined to understand her body, she immersed herself in the science of nutrition and human health, searching for answers beyond medication.

Following Her Passion

While completing her Pharmacy degree, she continued learning nutrition and wellness, preparing for the path she truly wanted to pursue.

Choosing Her Purpose

After marriage, she pursued a Master's in Nutrition and Dietetics, transforming a personal struggle into a professional mission.

The Transformation

In 2022, she embraced the Mātrāśin philosophy and lost 26 kg, gaining energy, strength, confidence and control over her health.

Motherhood & Resilience

Following childbirth, she faced Retinitis Pigmentosa and severe postpartum depression, yet continued rebuilding herself stronger than ever before.

The Gain-Gain Story

This Is Not A Story Of Loss

People often ask about Pragna's weight-loss journey. She gently corrects them—this is a story about what she gained.
